## Ingredients

- Raw onion , sliced, cut into rings, or diced (as much as you need for your recipe)
- Kosher or sea salt
- Red wine vinegar to cover


## Instructions

1. Put the onion in a non-reactive vessel along with a large pinch of salt. Pour enough vinegar on top to just cover. Let stand, stirring once or twice, for at least 15 minutes and up to 1 hour (the onion will grow softer and more pickled-tasting as it sits). Drain, then add to sandwiches, salads, and other dishes where raw onion would be used.

2. Serve and enjoy.
