# Cardamom Lemonade

## Ingredients

- 18 green cardamom pods (see notes)
- 3 pounds ( 1.3 kg ) room-temperature lemons ( 10 to 12 medium lemons )
- 14 ounces sugar ( 2 cups ; 400 g )
- 2 teaspoons green cardamom seeds (see notes)
- 24 ounces cold water ( 3 cups ; 700 ml )
- 1 teaspoon Diamond Crystal kosher salt , plus more to taste; for table salt, use half as much by volume


## Instructions

1. Lemon squeezer or juicer, mortar and pestle or spice grinder, cheesecloth or nonreactive fine-mesh strainer, 2-quart pitcher.

2. I prefer a tart lemonade, so this recipe calls for adding 8 ounces (1 cup) of reserved lemon juice to the lemon rind syrup. If you prefer your lemonade sweeter, start with 6 ounces of lemon juice and taste before adding more.

3. Green cardamom is generally considered sweeter and more versatile, suitable for both savory and sweet dishes, while black cardamom has a smokier, more robust flavor and is typically used in savory dishes. Do not substitute with black cardamom in this recipe.

4. If green cardamom seeds are unavailable, you can substitute with ground cardamom and omit pulverizing in step 3.

5. The concentrated lemonade can be refrigerated in an airtight nonreactive container for up to 1 week.
