## Ingredients

- 1 pound ( 454 g ) shiitake mushrooms , stemmed and sliced 1/2-inch thick
- 1 1/2 tablespoons ( 10 g ) smoked paprika
- Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
- 1/2 cup ( 120 ml ) extra-virgin olive oil


## Instructions

1. Roast mushrooms, using tongs or a wooden spoon to toss occasionally, until very well browned and edges are crispy, 35 to 40 minutes. Serve hot or at room temperature.

2. 10-inch cast iron skillet
