# Mayo-Free Chicken Salad With Kimchi, Ginger, and Scallions

> Mixing mayonnaise into chicken salad is the most common way to add moisture, but it&#39;s not the only way. Vinaigrette works well too, like this tangy Korean-inspired rendition with kimchi, pine nuts, and lots of fresh ginger.

## Ingredients

- 4 cups shredded cooked chicken meat (from about 1 ( 3-pound ) roast chicken)
- 1 cup drained kimchi , chopped
- 1/2 cup diced celery (about 1 large rib)
- 1/4 cup toasted pine nuts or sesame seeds
- 1/4 cup thinly sliced scallion , white and light green parts only (about 2 medium scallions)
- 3 tablespoons kimchi liquid
- 3 tablespoons distilled white vinegar
- 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 2 teaspoons grated fresh ginger
- 1 teaspoon toasted sesame oil
- Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
- Sliced bread and frisee, if serving as a sandwich


## Instructions

1. In a large bowl, toss together all ingredients to combine thoroughly. Season with salt and pepper and serve as a light salad or on bread as a sandwich. Salad can be refrigerated in an airtight container overnight.
