# Beurre d'Escargot (Garlic-and-Herb "Escargot" Butter)

> This exceedingly simple garlic-and-herb butter comes together in minutes—and instantly upgrades vegetables, chicken, bread, pasta, and more.

## Ingredients

- 10 medium cloves garlic ( 1 3/4 ounces ; 50 g )
- 1/4 medium shallot ( 10 g ), coarsely chopped
- 50 g ( 1 3/4 ounces ) flat-leaf parsley leaves , from 1 small bunch
- 1/2 teaspoon Diamond Crystal kosher salt ; for table salt, use half as much by volume
- Freshly ground black pepper
- 226 g unsalted butter ( 8 ounces ; 16 tablespoons ), cut into 1-inch cubes, at room temperature


## Instructions

1. Food processor unless making by hand

2. To make escargot butter by hand, finely chop garlic, shallot, and parsley leaves. In a medium bowl, combine garlic, shallot, parsley, salt, and a crack of black pepper. Add cubed butter and, using a wooden spoon or flexible spatula, mix until well-combined. Refrigerate in an airtight container.
