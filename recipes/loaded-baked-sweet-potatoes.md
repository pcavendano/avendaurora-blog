# Loaded Baked Sweet Potatoes

> Loaded up with melted cheddar, crispy bacon, minced chives, and sour cream, baked potatoes—whether you choose to use starchy russets or sweet garnets—are a simple and filling dinner.

## Ingredients

- 4 medium sweet potatoes (about 12 ounces ; 340 g each), washed, scrubbed, and dried
- Canola, vegetable, or extra-virgin olive oil , for rubbing
- 4 tablespoons unsalted butter ( 2 ounces ; 56 g ), diced
- Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
- Toppings of your choice, such as grated cheddar cheese , crispy diced bacon bits, minced chives, sour cream, and store-bought homemade chili


## Instructions

1. Adjust oven rack to middle position and preheat to 375ºF (190ºC). Using a fork or paring knife, puncture sweet potatoes in several spots. Rub each sweet potato all over with a light coating of oil. Arrange on a wire rack set on a rimmed baking sheet and bake until a fork can easily be inserted into the center of each sweet potato and skin is crisp and lightly browned, about 1 hour 15 minutes.

2. Let sweet potatoes rest 5 minutes. Slice each open lengthwise almost all the way through, open like a book, and scoop flesh into a mixing bowl. Add butter, season with salt and pepper, and using a potato masher, mash until butter is incorporated but potatoes are still slightly chunky.

3. Scoop mashed sweet potatoes back into skins. (You can refill each skin, making 4 smaller potatoes, or use all the mashed sweet potatoes to refill only half the skins, making 2 overstuffed sweet potatoes.) Using your hands, reshape each stuffed sweet potato back into a classic baked potato shape.

4. Top with toppings of your choice. To melt cheese, add it first, then return sweet potatoes to oven until cheese is melted, about 1 minute. If topping with chili, place about 1/2 cup chili onto potato, top with grated cheddar, then return stuffed sweet potatoes to oven until cheese is melted, about 1 minute. Serve right away.

5. If you’re baking four potatoes, you can refill each skin to make four smaller sweet potatoes, or fill two of the skins for two big, overstuffed sweet potatoes.

6. This recipe can be doubled.
