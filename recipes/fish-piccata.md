# Fish Piccata

> This incredibly easy recipe yields tender, pan-fried fish fillets with a bright, caper-studded lemon-butter sauce.

## Ingredients

- All-purpose flour , for dredging (about 1/2 cup )
- 4 ( 6 - ounce ; 170 g ) thin boneless, skinless white-fleshed fish fillets , about 1/2- to 3/4-inch thick, such as flounder , scrod , haddock , or branzino
- 1/4 tsp salt and freshly ground black pepper
- 60 ml vegetable oil
- 80 ml white wine
- 1 1/2 tablespoons ( 1/2 ounce ; 15 g ) drained capers in vinegar
- 4 tablespoons ( 60 g ) unsalted butter , cut into tablespoon-size pieces, plus 1 tablespoon ( 15 g ) unsalted butter mashed into a paste with 1/4 teaspoon all-purpose flour
- 1 tablespoon ( 15 ml ) fresh lemon juice from 1 whole lemon
- 1 1/2 tablespoons ( 1/4 ounce ; 8 g ) minced flat-leaf parsley leaves and tender stems
