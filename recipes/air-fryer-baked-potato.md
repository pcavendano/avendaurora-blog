# Air-Fryer Baked Potato

> In our potato-centric worldview, this air-fryer method makes the very best baked potatoes.

## Ingredients

- Two 8-ounce (227g) russet potatoes , unpeeled
- Canola , vegetable , or olive oil , for rubbing
- Kosher salt
- Freshly ground black pepper
- 4 tablespoons (57 g ) butter , softened
- Toppings of your choice, such as grated cheddar cheese , crispy diced bacon bits, minced chives , sliced scallions , and/or sour cream


## Instructions

1. Using a fork or paring knife, lightly prick each potato all over several times. Rub each potato with oil. Sprinkle evenly with salt and pepper.

2. Place potatoes in air fryer basket, spaced evenly apart. Place basket in air fryer and cook at 400°F (205°C) until the potato skin is crispy and a paring knife inserted into the center of the potatoes meets little to no resistance, about 45 minutes. (Center of potatoes should register between 205 to 210°F [96 to 99°C] with an instant-read thermometer.)

3. Transfer potatoes to a plate and let rest for 5 minutes. Slice each open lengthwise and scoop flesh into a mixing bowl. Add butter, season with salt and pepper, and roughly mash, just until butter is incorporated but potatoes are still chunky.

4. Add toppings of your choice. To melt cheese, add it first, then return potatoes to the air fryer set at 400°F oven until cheese melts, about 30 seconds to 1 minute; alternatively, use a torch to melt cheese. Serve right away.
