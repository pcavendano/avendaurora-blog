# Light and Frothy Raspberry Faluda

> This lighter recipe for faluda froths puréed raspberries with milk. You could sweeten it as much as you like, layer it with whipped cream, or spike it with ginger and cardamom. This lighter version just adds a garnish of candied ginger, which you could also dice up and add to the basil seed.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup (about 4 ounces ) raspberries , cold
- 1 cup whole milk , cold
- 2 tablespoons honey , or to taste
- 2 teaspoons basil seeds , soaked for 10 minutes in 6 tablespoons water
- 2 slices candied ginger , for garnish


## Instructions

1. Purée raspberries in blender until smooth, then blend in milk and honey until a light froth forms. Divide into two glasses, stir in soaked basil seeds, and garnish rim with a slice of candied ginger. Serve chilled.
