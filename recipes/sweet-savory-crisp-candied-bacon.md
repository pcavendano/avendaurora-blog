# Sweet, Savory, Crisp Candied Bacon

> This easy candied bacon recipe—sometimes called Millionaire’s Bacon—uses thick-cut slices, brown sugar, and spice for the ultimate crispy sweet-salty bite. Perfect for a festive brunch or crumbled over soup.

## Ingredients

- 6 center-cut bacon slices
- 2 tablespoons ( 26 g ) packed light brown sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- 1/4 teaspoon smoked paprika
- 1/8 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper


## Instructions

1. djust oven rack to middle position and preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a rimmed baking sheet with aluminum foil and top with a wire rack.

2. Rimmed backing sheet, aluminum foil, wire rack

3. This recipe can easily be doubled.
