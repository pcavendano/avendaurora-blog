# Walnut Skordalia (Easy 5-Ingredient Greek Garlic Dip)

> Greek walnut skordalia is a simple five-ingredient garlic dip that combines bread, walnuts, and olive oil into a bold, creamy spread that&#39;s ready in minutes.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup ( 45 to 55 g ) packed, bite-size pieces of stale bread
- 2 large cloves garlic , grated
- 1 tablespoon ( 15 ml ) red wine vinegar or white balsamic vinegar
- 1 teaspoon ( 5 g ) Diamond Crystal kosher salt , more to taste; for table salt use half as much by volume
- 125 g walnuts (about 1 ¼ cups ), plus more for decoration
- 1/3 cup ( 75 ml ) olive oil
- Ground pepper to taste
- Pita or crudites for serving


## Instructions

1. Stop here for a rustic, chunky version. For a smoother dip, continue below.

2. Measure 1/3 cup (80 ml) water. Add half of water to mixture and process for 5 to 10 seconds to combine. Periodically pause to scrape sides of blending jar as needed. Add remaining water, 1 tablespoon at a time, and process until a thick, dippable consistency is achieved. Add up to an additional 1 to 2 additional tablespoons (14 to 28 ml) of water for a looser consistency, if desired. Season to taste with salt, pepper, and vinegar.

3. Serve in a shallow bowl, drizzled with olive oil and topped with a walnut placed in the center for decoration. Serve with pita or crudites.

4. Food processor or high-powered blender

5. If you do not have stale bread, break up pieces of fresh bread and dry them in the oven at 325°F (160 °C) until lightly browned, 5 to 7 minutes. Let cool completely before using.
