# Air-Fryer Asparagus

> The air fryer is a fast and foolproof way to cook tender-crisp asparagus with a deep woodsy flavor.

## Ingredients

- 1 pound ( 450 g ) asparagus , trimmed (see note)
- 2 teaspoons extra-virgin olive oil
- 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
- 1/8 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper


## Instructions

1. Set air fryer to 400°F (205°C) and preheat for 5 minutes.

2. In a bowl, toss asparagus with oil, salt, and pepper. Working in batches if necessary, transfer asparagus to basket in a single layer, and cook until asparagus is browned in a few spots and tender-crisp, 5 to 10 minutes (see notes).

3. If asparagus is too long to fit in the air fryer, halve it crosswise.

4. I recommend using asparagus that is at least 1/2-inch thick. The cooking time may vary depending on the thickness of the asparagus

5. We recommend using a 6-quart or larger air fryer. If using a smaller air fryer, you may need to cook the asparagus in batches to ensure even cooking and spacing.
