# Grilled Potato Wedges

> These easy grilled potato wedges are crunchy on the outside, and fluffy on the inside.

## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1 teaspoon ancho chile (see notes)
- 1 teaspoon Diamond Crystal kosher salt ; for table salt, use half as much by volume
- 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- 3 large russet potatoes , scrubbed and cut into 1-inch thick wedges
- 1/3 cup ( 80 ml ) extra-virgin olive oil


## Instructions

1. Place cooked potato wedges in a large bowl. Sprinkle with spice mixture to taste and toss to evenly coat. Serve immediately.
