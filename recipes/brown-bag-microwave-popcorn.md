# Brown Bag Microwave Popcorn

> Make popcorn in the microwave in a brown paper bag—not the store-bought microwaveable stuff that comes pre-coated in flavors, but your own. A bag of microwave popcorn is incredibly easy to make at home and lets you customize your popcorn with your own flavors.

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup popcorn kernels
- 1/2 teaspoon canola or vegetable oil
- Kosher salt, to taste


## Instructions

1. In a small mixing bowl, add combine popcorn kernels, oil, and salt. Toss well until kernels are evenly coated in oil.

2. Transfer popcorn kernels to a clean brown paper lunch bag. Evenly fold top of bag over by approximately 1.5 inches and then fold each of the top corners of the bag over diagonally. Tear a small tab out of the center of each diagonal fold, and then fold that over as well. This prevents the bag from opening as the popcorn expands.

3. Place the kernel-filled brown paper bag in the microwave and run on high for about 2 minutes, watching carefully the entire time to ensure the bag doesn&#39;t smolder or, worse, catch fire. The exact timing will vary depending on the microwave, so be sure to err a little on the un-popped side of things to avoid scorching and burning the popcorn. Serve.

4. Brown paper lunch bag, microwave

5. As noted at the start of this recipe, brown paper bags are not necessarily designed to be microwave-safe. Watch the whole time when microwaving your popcorn, and if in doubt, it&#39;s better to err on the side of leaving some kernels unpopped rather than trying to pop every last one.
