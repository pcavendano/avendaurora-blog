# Bharat (Levantine Spice Mix)

> Learn how to make bharat, the all-purpose Levantine spice blend that adds warmth and depth to rice, meats, stews, and more.

## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon whole coriander seeds or 2 1/4 teaspoons ground coriander
- 1 tablespoon whole allspice berries or 2 1/4 teaspoons ground allspice
- 1 tablespoon green cardamom pods or 2 teaspoons freshly ground cardamom
- 1 1/2 teaspoons peppercorns or 1 teaspoon black pepper
- 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon (see notes)


## Instructions

1. Spice grinder or mortar and pestle

2. For the best results, use high-quality ground cinnamon, or grind your own if you have a powerful spice grinder.

3. This recipe can easily be scaled up to make a larger batch.

4. The amount of bharat used per dish depends on personal preference and the dish itself. It&#39;s traditionally used generously, especially in rice dishes and for seasoning ground meat.
