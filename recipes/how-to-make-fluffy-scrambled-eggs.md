# How to Make Fluffy Scrambled Eggs

> Do you have to add milk to scrambled eggs? No, but they keep the dish more flavorful and moist. Go on, give it a try.

## Ingredients

- 3 large eggs
- 1/8 teaspoon kosher salt (see note)
- 2 tablespoons ( 1 ounce ; 30 ml ) milk (optional)
- 1/2 tablespoon ( 1/4 ounce ; 8 g ) unsalted butter
- Freshly ground black or white pepper


## Instructions

1. In a small bowl, beat eggs with salt and milk (if using).

2. In a nonstick skillet, melt butter over medium-high heat until foamy. Add eggs and cook, stirring and scrambling gently with a silicone spatula, until large, fluffy curds form and eggs are fully cooked through, about 3 minutes. Season with pepper and serve.

3. We like to season our eggs with salt at about 1% of their weight, or about 1.5g for 3 large eggs (though we rarely actually measure it out in practice). Just note that not only do different eaters have different salt-level preferences, but the volume of a given weight of salt itself will vary dramatically by brand, so season at your own discretion.
