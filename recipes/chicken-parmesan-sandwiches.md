# Chicken Parmesan Sandwiches

> These juicy and flavorful chicken parm sandwiches can be made into individual subs or in a big party size to feed a crowd.

## Ingredients

- 1 large loaf ciabatta (for a party-size sandwich) or 6 ( 8- or 10-inch) hoagie rolls (for individual subs)
- Extra-virgin olive oil , for drizzling
- 1 1/2 cups red sauce (see notes)
- 1 batch Chicken Parmesan , fully cooked and warmed through
- 6 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese


## Instructions

1. Adjust broiler rack to 8 inches below the heat source and preheat broiler to high. Place bread cut-side-up on a foil-lined rimmed baking sheet and drizzle lightly all over with olive oil, then broil until well browned and crisp, about 1 minute.

2. Return to broiler and cook until cheese is fully melted and starting to bubble and brown, about 2 minutes. Remove from oven and immediately close sandwich, pressing down firmly to seal and let rest for 1 minute; alternatively, for the full Italian-deli experience, wrap the sandwich tightly in foil and let steam for 5 minutes before eating. For the party-size sandwich, slice into 6 to 8 single-serving pieces and serve.
