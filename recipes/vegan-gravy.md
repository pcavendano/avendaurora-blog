# Vegan Gravy

> This vegan gravy layers toasted mushroom powder, coconut milk, and soy sauce to build deep umami without any dairy or meat.

## Ingredients

- 1 3/4 ounces ( 50 g ) dried mushrooms , such as shiitake or porcini, frozen for at least 2 hours
- 2 tablespoons ( 30 ml ) refined coconut oil
- 4 sprigs fresh thyme
- 2 sprigs fresh rosemary
- 4 large fresh sage leaves
- 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- 1 large shallot ( 3 1/2 ounces ; 100 g ), thinly sliced
- 4 cloves garlic , smashed and peeled
- 1 cup ( 240 ml ) full-fat canned coconut milk , mixed well
- 1 cup ( 240 ml ) store-bought or homemade vegetable brot h, plus more as needed
- 2 tablespoons ( 30 ml ) soy sauce or tamari, plus more as needed (see notes)
- 1/4 teaspoon dark soy sauce , optional (see notes)
- 2 teaspoons ( 10 ml ) Dijon mustard
- 1/4 teaspoon freshly grated nutmeg , optional


## Instructions

1. Spice grinder, medium saucepan, fine-mesh strainer

2. Opt for tamari over soy sauce to keep the gravy gluten-free. (While most tamari, Dijon mustards, and broths are gluten-free, it’s important to check the label if gluten is a concern.)

3. Using dark soy sauce gives the gravy a deeper color. Omit or use a gluten-free brand to keep the gravy gluten-free.

4. The gravy can be made up to 4 days in advance. Store in an airtight container in the refrigerator. Reheat gently in a small saucepan over low heat, whisking occasionally, until hot and smooth. If needed, whisk in a splash of vegetable broth or water to restore consistency.
