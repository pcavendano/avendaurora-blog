# Air-Fried Sweet Potato Fries

> Sweet potatoes fries are notoriously floppy, mushy, and soggy. But our recipe incorporates a secret technique that yields crispy-on-the-outside, tender-on-the-inside results.

## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons oil
- 2 teaspoons cornstarch
- 2 teaspoons white rice flour, such as Bob’s Red Mill
- 1/2 teaspoon granulated sugar
- 1 teaspoon kosher salt, divided; for table salt, use half as much by volume
- 1 large sweet potato (about 1 pound; 425g), peeled and cut into 1/4-inch thick fries
- Nonstick cooking spray


## Instructions

1. Preheat a 6-quart air fryer to 385°F (196ºC) for 5 minutes. In a medium bowl, whisk together oil, cornstarch, rice flour, sugar, and 1/2 teaspoon salt until a smooth slurry paste consistency. Add sweet potato and toss to evenly coat. Pour into basket of air fryer (reserving medium bowl). Cook, shaking basket and spraying fries generously with cooking spray every 5 to 6 minutes, until starting to brown, about 13 minutes. Reduce air fryer temperature to 375ºF (190ºC). Continue to cook, shaking basket and spraying fries generously with cooking spray every 5 minutes, until crispy and browned, about 10 more minutes.

2. Meanwhile, clean and dry the reserved medium bowl, and set aside. Remove basket from air fryer, and pour fries into reserved bowl. Add remaining 1/2 teaspoon salt, and toss evenly to coat. Pour onto a serving platter or basket, and serve immediately.
