# Brazilian Lemonade (With Limes and Condensed Milk)

> Brazilian-style lemonade is made with fresh lime juice and sweetened with condensed milk. It&#39;s a richer, sweeter version of the lemonade we&#39;re used to, and excellent with a shot of Cachaça.

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup juice from about 10 limes or 5 lemons
- 3/4 cup sweetened condensed milk
- Pinch salt
- 3 cups cold water
- 1 quart ice


## Instructions

1. Combine lemon or lime juice, condensed milk, salt, and water in a blender. Blend until homogenous and frothy, about 30 seconds. Transfer to a pitcher. Add ice. Serve immediately.
