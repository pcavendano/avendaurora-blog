# Jus Alpukat (Indonesian Avocado-Coffee Shake)

> Equally perfect as your morning beverage or after-dinner dessert, this rich and creamy avocado shake is an absolutely delicious way to get your daily dose of caffeine.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup ( 240 ml ) whole milk , plus more as needed
- 1/2 cup ( 120 ml ) sweetened condensed milk
- 2 medium Hass avocados (about 8 ounces / 225 g each), halved, pitted, and flesh scooped
- 10 ounces ( 285 g ) ice (about 10 ice cubes )
- 1/4 cup ( 60 ml ) strongly brewed coffee (such as a double espresso shot )
- Pinch kosher or sea salt
- Chocolate syrup , for drizzling (optional)


## Instructions

1. In a blender, combine whole milk, sweetened condensed milk, avocado, ice, coffee, and salt. Blend on high speed until smooth, 30 seconds to 1 minute; if the shake is thicker than you&#39;d like, blend in more milk to thin slightly.

2. If desired, drizzle chocolate syrup on inside of serving glasses, then divide shake evenly between glasses, and serve.

3. This shake is also delicious without the coffee.

4. The shake should be made right before serving.
