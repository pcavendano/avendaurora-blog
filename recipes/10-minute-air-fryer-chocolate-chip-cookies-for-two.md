# 10-Minute Air-Fryer Chocolate Chip Cookies for Two

> Get warm, melty chocolate chip cookies in minutes with this small-batch air-fryer recipe—ideal for immediate snacking needs.

## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup all-purpose flour ( 3.375 ounces ; 95 g )
- 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/8 teaspoon kosher salt
- 4 tablespoons unsalted butter , melted and cooled slightly
- 1/2 cup dark or light brown sugar
- 1 large egg yolk
- 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1/3 cup dark or milk chocolate chips


## Instructions

1. In a small bowl, using a small whisk or fork, whisk flour, baking soda, and salt together. In a medium bowl, whisk butter and sugar vigorously until sugar is fully dissolved, about 1 minute. Vigorously whisk egg and vanilla into butter mixture until fully combined and mixture is glossy, about 1 minute.

2. Using a rubber spatula or spoon, stir flour mixture into butter mixture until just combined, then stir in chocolate chips. Cover and refrigerate for 5 to 10 minutes, until batter is slightly thickened.

3. Using wet hands (to avoid sticking), divide dough into 6 equal portions and roll into balls; set on counter or a clean plate. Preheat 6-quart air fryer at 325℉ (160℃) for 3 minutes. Line preheated air fryer basket with parchment paper.

4. Set dough balls on the parchment paper in air-fryer basket, spaced apart, making sure some are close to the perimeter of the parchment paper (this ensures the parchment won&#39;t flap over the cookie edges while baking). Bake at 325℉ (160℃) until edges of cookies are set and beginning to brown but centers are still soft and puffy, 6 to 10 minutes. Let cool on parchment for 10 minutes. Serve.
