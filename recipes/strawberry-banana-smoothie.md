# Strawberry Banana Smoothie

> Made with just a handful of ingredients, this strawberry banana smoothie tastes like a sippable version of strawberries and cream.

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups frozen strawberries ( 10 ounces ; 290 g )
- 1 1/2 cups ( 355 ml ) whole milk (see note)
- 1 ripe banana
- 1 tablespoon strawberry jam ( 1 ounce ; 25 g )


## Instructions

1. Combine frozen strawberries, milk, banana, and strawberry jam in a blender. Pulse a few times to break up the fruit, then blend on high speed until completely smooth, about 1 minute.

2. Feel free to substitute with dairy-free milk if desired.
