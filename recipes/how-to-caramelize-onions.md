# How to Caramelize Onions

> This recipe for classic caramelized onions couldn&#39;t be easier. Just slice some onions and add butter and time.

## Ingredients

- 6 tablespoons ( 90 g ) unsalted butter
- 3 pounds ( 1.4 kg ) yellow or mixed onions , sliced 1/8 inch thick (see note)
- Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper


## Instructions

1. In a large stainless steel sauté pan, or in 2 large stainless steel or cast iron skillets, melt butter over medium-high heat until foaming. Add onions and cook, stirring occasionally, until softened, about 8 minutes. Lower heat to medium-low and cook, stirring frequently, until onions are very sweet and a rich golden-brown color, 1 to 2 hours. If browned onion juices on bottom of pot threaten to burn, add 1 tablespoon (15ml) water, scrape up browned bits, and continue cooking; add water whenever needed to prevent burning. Season with salt and pepper.

2. Yellow onions are your best all-purpose bet, but a mix of yellow, sweet (e.g., Vidalia), and red onions, as well as shallots, produces an even more complex flavor.

3. The onions can be refrigerated in a sealed container for up to one week. Rewarm before serving.
