> For the best roasted sweet potato, freeze it first.

## Ingredients

- Four 1-pound (454 g) sweet potatoes , scrubbed (see note)
- Thyme , rosemary , sage , or other aromatics (optional)


## Instructions

1. For the Easier Method: Freeze the sweet potatoes for as long as you can (if you don&#39;t have time, proceed with cooking). Place them onto a foil-lined baking sheet.

2. Place on the center rack of a cold oven and turn the oven on to 425ºF (220ºC) and cook until fork tender, about 1 hour. If you have a little more time, you can start at 300ºF (150ºC) and cook the sweet potatoes for 30 minutes to an hour, before cranking the oven temperature to 425ºF (220ºC) to finish.
