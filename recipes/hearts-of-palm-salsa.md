# Hearts of Palm Salsa

> Inspired by salada de palmito, a popular side dish in Central and South America, this fresh tomato salsa has creamy, tender hearts of palm.

## Ingredients

- 1 pound ( 450 g ) ripe plum tomatoes (about 5 whole tomatoes ), cut into 1/4-inch dice
- 1 teaspoon ( 4 g ) Diamond Crystal kosher salt , plus more for seasoning; for table salt, use half as much by volume or equal amount by weight
- 1 (14-ounce; 400 g) can hearts of palm , drained and cut into 1/4-inch dice
- 1/4 medium red onion (about 2 ounces ; 60 g ), finely diced
- 2 medium garlic cloves ( 10 g ), finely grated or minced
- 1 jalapeño chile (about 1 1/2 ounces ; 45 g ), stemmed, seeded, and finely diced
- 1/4 cup ( 12 g ) roughly chopped fresh cilantro leaves
- 1 tablespoon ( 15 ml ) fresh lime juice from 1 whole lime
- Tortilla chips , for serving (optional)


## Instructions

1. In a large bowl, add tomatoes and salt and toss to combine. Transfer tomatoes to a fine-mesh strainer or colander and set inside large bowl. Let drain for 30 minutes; discard liquid and wipe out bowl.

2. In now-empty bowl, add drained tomatoes, hearts of palm, onion, garlic, jalapeño, cilantro, and lime juice; mix until thoroughly combined.

3. Serve immediately with tortilla chips, or transfer to an airtight container and refrigerate for up to 4 days.
