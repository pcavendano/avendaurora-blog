# Pickleback

> A staple of dive bars around the world, the pickleback is a simple shot combo that sees whiskey (these days typically Jameson) followed immediately by pickle brine. It&#39;s a salty, vinegary, palate cleansing combo invented at the Bushwick Country Club and we spoke with the folks who were there when it was invented.

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 fluid ounces ( 44 ml ) whiskey of your choosing
- 1 1/2 fluid ounces ( 44 ml ) chilled, strained pickle brine of your choosing


## Instructions

1. Pour whiskey into a small serving vessel such as a shot glass, and pour pickle brine into an identical glass. Drink whiskey as a shot followed immediately by pickle brine.

2. This recipe is adaptable: you can pair spirits and brines of all types. It may take some experimentation to find a flavor combination that works for you, though.
