# Roasted Chicken Thighs With Bacon, Apples, and Cabbage

> This easy sheet-pan dinner tastes like the very best of fall. Crisp, juicy chicken thighs are cooked alongside bacon, apples, and cabbage, then dressed in a simple pan sauce that gets sweetness from maple syrup and brightness from apple cider vinegar.

## Ingredients

- 4 slices thick-cut bacon ( 4 ounces ; 113 g ), cut into 1/2-inch pieces (see notes)
- 1 small head green cabbage (about 2 pounds ; 906 g ), cored and cut into 1-inch thick wedges
- 1 1/2 pounds ( 680 g ) bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
- Kosher salt and black pepper, to taste
- 1 large Honeycrisp or other firm, sweet apple (about 8 ounces ; 227 g ), cored and cut into 1-inch pieces
- 2 tablespoons ( 30 ml ) extra-virgin olive oil
- 3 tablespoons ( 45 ml ) apple cider vinegar
- 1 tablespoon ( 15 ml ) maple syrup
- Crusty bread , for serving (optional)
- Cooked farro , couscous, or roasted potatoes for serving (optional)


## Instructions

1. Pour pan sauce all over chicken, cabbage, and apples, and serve with bread, couscous, or roasted potatoes, if desired.

2. 13- by-18-inch rimmed baking sheet, instant-read thermometer, fish spatula

3. Lardons can be used in place of bacon.

4. Leftovers can be refrigerated in an airtight container for up to 3 days.
