# Salmoriglio

> This Sicilian sauce is bright, packed with fresh herbs, and can be used as both a marinade and a sauce.

## Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon finely grated lemon zest plus 1/4 cup ( 60 ml ) lemon juice from 2 medium lemons
- Kosher salt and freshly-ground black pepper
- 3/4 cup ( 180 ml ) extra-virgin olive oil
- 2 garlic cloves , minced
- 3/4 cup fresh oregano leaves ( 3/4 ounce ; 21 g ), finely minced
- 3/4 cup fresh parsley leaves ( 3/4 ounce ; 21 g ), finely minced


## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, whisk lemon zest, juice, 3/4 teaspoon salt, and 1/2 teaspoon pepper together.

2. Stir in oregano and parsley. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

3. Microwave-safe liquid measuring cup, microwave
