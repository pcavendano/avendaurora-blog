Tips:

- If you’d like to skip the poaching step and substitute rotisserie chicken, add 2 teaspoons ground cumin and 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper along with the flour and chili powder in step 5.
- A combination of Monterey Jack and cheddar cheese can be used, if desired.
- The casserole can be made 1 day in advance. Prepare casserole through step 6. Let cool slightly, uncovered, at room temperature for 1 hour. Cover with aluminum foil and refrigerate up to 24 hours. When ready to serve, uncover and let casserole come to room temperature for 30 minutes while oven preheats to 400ºF (205ºC). Bake, uncovered, until bubbling and browned and casserole is heated through, about 25 minutes, loosely covering with aluminum foil to prevent overbrowning, if needed. Garnish and serve as directed.
- Leftovers can be refrigerated in an airtight container for up to 3 days, but the tortillas will soften as they sit.
