Title: Chicken Couscous Tfaya (Moroccan Couscous With Braised Chicken)

----

Category: platos-fuertes, sopas-caldos

----

Description: Chicken couscous t’faya is a classic Moroccan dish featuring fluffy couscous piled with tender braised chicken topped with a rich, caramelized onion sauce. This signature onion sauce, t’faya, is sweetened with raisins and flavored with cinnamon and honey to create a balance of savory and sweet flavors.
