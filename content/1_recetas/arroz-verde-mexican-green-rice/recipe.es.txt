  instruction: Reheat remaining oil in Dutch oven over medium heat. Add rice and cook, stirring constantly, until the grains are lightly toasted, about 5 minutes. Add blended mixture to rice and stir to coat.

  instruction: Add remaining 1 cup (240 ml) stock or broth. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat then reduce heat to medium-low to maintain a simmer. Using a wooden spatula, scrape the bottom of the pan multiple times to ensure no rice is stuck. Remove from heat and rest, covered, at least 10 minutes or up to 20 minutes. Don&#39;t uncover or agitate the rice while resting.
