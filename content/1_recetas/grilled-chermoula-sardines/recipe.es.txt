  instruction: In a large bowl, whisk together olive oil, cilantro, parsley, lemon juice, garlic, paprika, cumin, harissa (if using), and salt until well combined. In a separate small bowl, set aside et 1/4 cup chermoula for serving.
  tip: ""
  step_image: 
-
  step_title: ""
  instruction: For a Charcoal Grill: Open bottom vent completely. Light large chimney starter filled 3/4 with charcoal briquettes (4 quarts). When top coals are partially covered with ash, pour evenly over bottom of grill grate. Set cooking grate in place, cover, and open lid vent completely. Heat grill until hot, about 5 minutes.
  tip: ""
  step_image: 
-
  step_title: ""
  instruction: For a Gas Grill: Turn all burners to high; cover; and heat grill until hot, about 15 minutes. Leave all burners on high.
  tip: ""
  step_image: 
-
  step_title: ""
  instruction: Lightly oil a grill basket to prevent sticking. Arrange prepared sardines in grill basket. Grill, flipping once, until skin is crisp and lightly charred and flesh is cooked through but still moist, 2 to 4 minutes per side, depending on size.
