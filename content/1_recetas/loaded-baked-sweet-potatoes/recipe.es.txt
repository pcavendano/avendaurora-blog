Instructions:
-
  step_title: ""
  instruction: Adjust oven rack to middle position and preheat to 375ºF (190ºC). Using a fork or paring knife, puncture sweet potatoes in several spots. Rub each sweet potato all over with a light coating of oil. Arrange on a wire rack set on a rimmed baking sheet and bake until a fork can easily be inserted into the center of each sweet potato and skin is crisp and lightly browned, about 1 hour 15 minutes.
  tip: ""
  step_image: 
-
  step_title: ""
  instruction: Let sweet potatoes rest 5 minutes. Slice each open lengthwise almost all the way through, open like a book, and scoop flesh into a mixing bowl. Add butter, season with salt and pepper, and using a potato masher, mash until butter is incorporated but potatoes are still slightly chunky.
  tip: ""
  step_image: 
-
  step_title: ""
  instruction: Scoop mashed sweet potatoes back into skins. (You can refill each skin, making 4 smaller potatoes, or use all the mashed sweet potatoes to refill only half the skins, making 2 overstuffed sweet potatoes.) Using your hands, reshape each stuffed sweet potato back into a classic baked potato shape.
  tip: ""
  step_image: 
-
  step_title: ""
  instruction: Top with toppings of your choice. To melt cheese, add it first, then return sweet potatoes to oven until cheese is melted, about 1 minute. If topping with chili, place about 1/2 cup chili onto potato, top with grated cheddar, then return stuffed sweet potatoes to oven until cheese is melted, about 1 minute. Serve right away.
  tip: ""
  step_image: 
-
  step_title: ""
  instruction: If you’re baking four potatoes, you can refill each skin to make four smaller sweet potatoes, or fill two of the skins for two big, overstuffed sweet potatoes.
  tip: ""
  step_image: 
-
  step_title: ""
  instruction: This recipe can be doubled.
