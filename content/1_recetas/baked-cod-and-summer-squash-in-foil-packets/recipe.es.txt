Instructions:
-
  step_title: ""
  instruction: Preheat the oven to 450°F (230°C). Season fish with salt and pepper. Arrange 4 large sheets of aluminum foil on a work surface and set 1 fish fillet on each piece of foil. Arrange zucchini, squash, and onion around each fillet and season with salt and pepper. Sprinkle fish and vegetables with red chile flakes. Drizzle 1/2 tablespoon oil and 1/2 tablespoon wine onto vegetables and fish.
  tip: ""
  step_image: 
-
  step_title: ""
  instruction: Arrange a few lemon slices on top of each fillet and top with a thyme sprig. Wrap foil around fish and vegetables, leaving some air space within each packet, and seal very well. Set packets on a rimmed baking sheet and bake for 8 minutes. Carefully open 1 packet to confirm fish is just cooked through; if not, re-seal packet and return to oven to continue cooking until cooked through. Serve right away, opening packets at the table.
  tip: ""
