Instructions:
-
  step_title: ""
  instruction: In a large Dutch oven, heat oil over medium-high until shimmering. Add onion and carrots. Cook, stirring occasionally, until softened, 3 to 5 minutes. Stir in garlic, ginger, cumin, pepper, turmeric, and smoked paprika, and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute. Add cabbage and cook, stirring often, until cabbage turns bright green, about 2 minutes.
