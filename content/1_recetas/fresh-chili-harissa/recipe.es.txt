  instruction: Preheat broiler. Line rimmed baking sheet with foil and set bell, poblano, fresno, and serrano peppers on it. Rub peppers with oil. Place under broiler and cook peppers, rotating as needed, until evenly charred all over, about 10 minutes. Transfer charred peppers to large bowl and cover with plastic wrap to steam.
