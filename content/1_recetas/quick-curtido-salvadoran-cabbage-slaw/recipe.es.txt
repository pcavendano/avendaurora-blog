----

Category: desayunos, platos-fuertes

Description: What sauerkraut is to a dog and kimchi is to a Korean pancake, curtido is to a variety of Latin American dishes. Traditional curtido is prepared days in advance of when it&#39;ll be eaten, enabling it to develop a fermented tang. This shortcut version is a nice fallback when you don&#39;t have time to think ahead.
