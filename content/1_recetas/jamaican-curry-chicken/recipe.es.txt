Tips:

- We like to add ground cumin, coriander seed, and garam masala to Jamaican curry powder for additional depth of flavor, but it&#39;s not required, and not something many Jamaican cooks would do. Feel free to omit those additional spices, if desired.
- Potatoes and carrots are something some cooks in Jamaica add to their curry chicken, while others don&#39;t. Our personal preference is not to include them, but we&#39;ve left them as optional here in case you&#39;re a fan.
- The curry chicken can be refrigerated in an airtight container for up to 2 days.
