Title: Hummus Kawarma (Lamb) with Lemon Sauce from 'Jerusalem'

----

Category: antojitos

----

Description: Here&#39;s a tahini-rich recipe for hummus along with two variations, Musabaha (warm chickpea topping) and Kawarma (fried chopped lamb). For the most show-stopping of hummus dishes, take the extra time and prepare the Kawarma. Freshly chopped lamb neck meat is marinated in warm spices, za&#39;atar, vinegar, mint, and parsley before it&#39;s fried in butter.

Instructions:
-
  step_title: ""
  instruction: To make the kawarma, place all the ingredients apart from the butter or ghee and oil in a medium bowl. Mix well, cover, and allow the mixture to marinate in the fridge for 30 minutes.
  tip: ""
  step_image: 
-
  step_title: ""
  instruction: Just before you are ready to cook the meat, place all the ingredients for the lemon sauce in a small bowl and stir well.
  tip: ""
  step_image: 
-
  step_title: ""
  instruction: Heat the butter or ghee and the olive oil in a large frying pan over medium-high heat. Add the meat in two or three batches and stir as you fry each batch for 2 minutes. The meat should be light pink in the middle.
  tip: ""
  step_image: 
-
  step_title: ""
  instruction: Divide the hummus among 6 individual shallow bowls, leaving a slight hollow in the center of each. Spoon the warm kawarma into the hollow and scatter with the reserved chickpeas. Drizzle generously with the lemon sauce and garnish with some parsley and the pine nuts.
