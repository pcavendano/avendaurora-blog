Title: Crispy Cheese Tacos

----

Category: antojitos

----

Description: Grated cheese is a common taco topping, but also a boring one if you fail to take advantage of the cheese&#39;s best assets: its melting and crisping abilities. The solution is here with these crispy cheese tacos, a fusion of melted, crisped cheese and tortilla that can be then stuffed with all sorts of fillings.
