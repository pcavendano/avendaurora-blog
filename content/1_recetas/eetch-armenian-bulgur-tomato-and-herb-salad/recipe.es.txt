  step_title: ""
  instruction: The texture of eetch is best when fine bulgur (“#1” grade) is used. If fine bulgur is unavailable, medium (#2) or coarse (#3) will work, although the results will be correspondingly more coarse and grainy. With other grades, be sure to use an equivalent amount by weight (175g), not volume.
