Instructions:
-
  step_title: ""
  instruction: Combine chicken, poblano peppers, tomatillos, onion, Anaheim peppers, Serrano peppers, garlic, cumin, and a big pinch of salt in a pressure cooker. Heat over high heat until gently sizzling, then seal pressure cooker, bring to high pressure, and cook for 15 minutes. Release pressure.
  tip: ""
  step_image: 
-
  step_title: ""
  instruction: Using tongs, transfer chicken pieces to a bowl and set aside. Add cilantro and fish sauce to remaining contents of pressure cooker. Blend with an immersion blender or in a countertop blender and season to taste with salt. Return chicken to sauce, discarding skin and bones and shredding if desired.
  tip: ""
  step_image: 
-
  step_title: ""
  instruction: Transfer to a serving platter, garnish with chopped cilantro, and serve immediately with tortillas and lime wedges.
